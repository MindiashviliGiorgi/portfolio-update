GM Railway

I used a form divided into several components, which were connected to each other through LocalStorage. I sent the final result as a complete ticket to Firebase, and from there, users could obtain a specific ticket using a unique reference number, allowing them to cancel or delete the ticket. Also you can convert final ticket as a PDF file for save information. The website is full responsive.

Travel More

Website is for people who love to travel and want to book hotels online. This website is Full-Stack work Angular/Firebase. You can log in and register on our website and you can also add your hotels which means you will be able to use admin panel system. Working search and all single details. Full website is responsive on the iPhone 12 Pro.

Gaming Web

On this website you can purchase computer games. You can register your account then log in and log out anytime you want. Also you'll see a small header which can help you to search the product by it's price, name or year of release. When you click on the product name you will get the full information about the game. If you want to purchase a product you will need to log in first then search for the game and add it to cart so you can pay. Web is full responsive The idea and design of the site is entirely my own.

Football Shop

The website is for an online store for football items, Find the desired product, add it to the cart and finally buy it, Both the idea and design of the website are full responsive including 375px, 1 used Angular(HTML5, SCSS, TypeScript, Firebase) to build a site. There is a header on the product pages, which makes it easier to choose the desired item According to brand, name and price increase or lack. We can add product to cart from product pages and also delete it, as you wish.
